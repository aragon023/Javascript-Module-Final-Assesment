build Toolkit

Welcome to my curated collection of interactive digital cards designed to support creative thinking, problem solving, and strategic exploration. Each card features a practical framework drawn from the fields of innovation, business, and design—ranging from classic models to contemporary methods. Whether you're brainstorming new ideas, mapping out customer journeys, or refining business strategies, these tools provide a structured approach to help you tackle challenges from different angles and unlock new possibilities.

This space is meant to be hands-on and adaptable. The cards are not just informative—they're built to be applied directly to your work, allowing you to engage with each method dynamically. From design thinking canvases to business model tools, the Toolkit serves as a practical resource for individuals and teams looking to approach problems more holistically, creatively, and effectively.
